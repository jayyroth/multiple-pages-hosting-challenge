History of Black Cats

Black cats have earned the title of misfortune & bad luck, as well as people associating them with evil & Satanic worship. However, this wasn't always the brand of the Black Cat. In ancient Egypt, cats were worshipped; in Celtic Mythology, a sign of a Black Cat signified prosperity; and in Britain & Japan, Black Cats are actually a sign of good luck.
